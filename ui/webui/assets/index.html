How Shell Gym works

Shell Gym trains command-line skills through short, hands-on exercises. Every exercise is completed in your terminal - this page only tells you what to do and watches your progress.

  1. Read the exercise on this page. It explains one small technique and gives you a concrete goal.
  2. Switch to the terminal and do it for real. Type the commands yourself - that is the whole point of the gym.
  3. Watch the check pass. Each task box verifies your work automatically. There is no "check" button: the moment the goal is reached, the spinner turns into a green check mark.
  4. Stuck? Keep trying - context-aware hints show up in the task box when the checks see you struggling.

The bar under the header tracks your overall progress. With auto-advance on, the next exercise opens by itself once you finish the current one; use the arrow buttons to move around manually.
